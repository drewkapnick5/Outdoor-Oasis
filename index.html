Our Story

Outdoor Oasis Design & Build is a dream 25 years in the making for CEO and founder Tyler Gilson. Growing up in Southeast Michigan and helping his dad and uncle at his uncle's garden center and landscaping company, he developed a love for the green industry. Though his love began with plants it gradually changed to a love for creating unique outdoor living spaces. After obtaining a degree from Michigan State University in Horticulture: Landscape Design, Construction and Management, Tyler decided to spread his wings and work in the industry for a few years. With a son on the way and a growing family, Tyler decided to move home and open Outdoor Oasis Design & Build. As your point of contact throughout the entire design and installation process, Tyler takes great pride in creating an outdoor living space that you, your family, and your loved ones will be able to enjoy for years to come. Nothing gives him more pride than building a space where friends become family and families are loved.
